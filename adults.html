Church is definitely not just for kids! Our adults, married, singles, young and old alike can take part in a bunch of activities.

  • There is great worship and Bible teaching every Sunday morning during the morning celebrations
  • Mid-week Bible study
  • Social events around football games, dominoes, euchre, etc.
  • Small group studies in homes or at the church
  • Eating in one another's homes or going out to eat with friends
  • Participating in occasional events to benefit those in need
  • Annual Bar-B-Q
  • And much more.........

    • There is a place for you here at FCC!

    Want to do more than simply attend? Then get involved on one of our many MINISTRY TEAMS.
