FCC participates relationally and financially with several different missionaries and ministries in a variety of countries around the world. Giving for some is accomplished as a line item in our annual church budget, others as special offerings, and still others as individual families in the church decide to partner with a given missionary.

Here are some of the organizations and individuals with whom we participate (not all are listed for security reasons):

Andrew & Anna
Mexico

Serving with Back 2 Back Ministries in Mexico working with at-risk children and women.

Aaron & Candace
Costa Rica

Serving with World Missions & Evangelism at the Abraham Project in ministries to at-risk children.

Studio Ten Ministries
South Asia

Personnel with Studio Ten Ministries working in church planting, education, and ministries to at-risk children and women.

Gabo & Emily
Mexico

Serving with Commission to Every Nation in Mexico in ministries to university students.

Global Teams
Costa Rica

Personnel with Global Teams in Costa Rica raising up Latinos to serve in the Middle East.

Paul & Carlene
Poland

Serving with SEND International in Poland working in church planting.

Dr. Randy Smith
Worldwide

Lead Pastor at Faith Community Church, ministering in a very part-time capacity as a pastoral friend to several cross-cultural missionaries. Faith Community Church also sends out occasional short-term mission teams. Visit his website www.globalmissionsonline.org

Cornerstone International
Costa Rica

Child sponsorship in Costa Rica.

Compassion International
Indonesia

Child sponsorship in Indonesia.

We see ourselves as only one of Michiana's great churches serving a great God in great cities. Through His people God spreads His message of salvation and love in Jesus Christ to a needy world. We are evangelical and rely on the Holy Spirit's empowering to engage in other's lives both locally and globally.
