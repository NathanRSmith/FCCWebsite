    Here at FCC people don't just receive, they give. The ministries of the church are carried out under the umbrella of a team. Many team leaders then represent those groups on the church's general board. See where you could fit!

    Prayer
    The focus of this team is on praying for the needs of the congregation and requests that have been submitted. They meet as a group as well of with those seeking soaking prayer.
    Outreach
    This lively team brainstorms activities that might appeal to those outside the church, aka "Servant Evangelism." It plans non-threatening events to which we can invite friends. Outreach also helps administer our benevolence focus.
    Adult Christian Education
    Adult CE covers adult Bible classes, small group studies, and discipleship ministries.
    Youth
    This group assists the youth pastor in carrying out ministry among our youth.
    Pastoral
    These individuals are on-call to assist the Lead Pastor in counseling and deliverance ministries.
    Social
    This team is responsible for ministering to the needs of the congregation in times of stress or celebration. For instance, they not only plan baby and wedding showers, but funeral dinners and meals for people recovering from illness.
    Trustee
    These folks take care of our physical building, grounds, and vehicle. They meet regularly for fellowship and "fix it" sessions.
    Children Christian Education
    This department is actually composed of a variety of teams covering children's church, children's Sunday School, nursery, mid-week Kids Klub, Vacation Bible School, and all things kids. Many people have found their place helping with games and refreshments, or even teaching a lesson.
    Worship
    The worship team includes vocalists, instrumentalist, sound and graphic technicians. The group meets on a rotating basis twice weekly for practices.
