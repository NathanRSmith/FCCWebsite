    FCC participates relationally and financially with several different missionaries and ministries in a variety of countries around the world. Giving for some is accomplished as a line item in our annual church budget, others as special offerings, and still others as individual families in the church decide to partner with a given missionary.

    Here are some of the organizations and individuals with whom we participate (not all are listed for security reasons):

    • Candace & Aaron (Brown) Espinoza in Costa Rica serving with World Missions & Evangelism at the Abraham Project in ministries to at-risk children.
    • Emily & Gabo (Bastine) Hernandez (emilybastine.blogspot.com) in Mexico serving with Campus Crusade in ministries to university students.
    • Jared & Jalayne Coblentz (jjcoblentz.blogspot.com) in Haiti serving with Christian Fellowship Mission in ministries orphans and unwed mothers.
    • Tom & Lori Kennedy in Costa Rica serving with Go to Nations in ministries to the Cabecar Indians and Costa Rican campers.
    • Paul & Carlene Eager (eagerlyblogging.blogspot.com) in Poland serving with SEND International in church planting.
    • Personnel with Studio Ten Ministries in Nepal working in ministries to at-risk children and women through local churches.
    • Personnel with Global Teams in Pakistan working in medical ministries through the national church.
    • Cornerstone International child sponsorship in Costa Rica.
    • Compassion International child sponsorship in Indonesia.
    • Dr. Randy Smith (www.globalmissionsonline.org), Lead Pastor at Faith Community Church, ministering in a very part-time capacity as a pastoral friend to several cross-cultural missionaries. Faith Community Church also sends out occasional short-term mission teams.
